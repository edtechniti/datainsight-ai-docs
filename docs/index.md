## Quick Example

Upload a CSV file, and within seconds you'll see:

- **Automatic insights** about your data patterns and trends
- **Smart chart recommendations** tailored to your dataset

## Why DataInsight AI?

Traditional data analysis tools are complex and require technical expertise. DataInsight AI makes data visualization accessible to everyone:

- **No installation required** - works directly in your browser
- **No learning curve** - intuitive interface guided by AI
- **No data upload** - your files stay on your device
- **No cost** - completely free to use (bring your own Claude API key)
